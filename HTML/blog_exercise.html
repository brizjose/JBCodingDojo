The Massey Ferguson 180 Diesel Tractor

The Massey Ferguson 180 Tractor was built between 1967 and 1975. It came with a Perkins four cylinder 3.9L diesel engine. Originally priced at $7,600 one can get it at probably $4,000 at a local auction. I totally recommend it. It is a really smooth ride, the engine is very quiet and strong, you'd think you are driving a gasoline vehicle. I love driving it around the farm, either hauling the manure spreader or the grass cutter.

Technical specifications:

Item Detail
