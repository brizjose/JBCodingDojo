Policy implementation

One of the most critical aspects of risk management policy implementation is the design of workflows to counter balance behavioral bias. Continuing education programs can correct for cognitive bias, but emotional bias is harder to work with both at the level of RM Committees and RM Teams.

Active management

Market timing and risk management can go hand in hand with the right systems in place. Firms with proprietary valuation tools and clear technical analysis signals have successfully implemented active risk management under their risk management policies. Hedge counterparts and rating agencies consider active risk management a credit risk mitigating factor when it acts under the umbrella of a well defined risk management policy.

IFRS 9

The international accountancy board has approved passage of IFRS 9, impacting recognition and derecognition of financial assets and liabilities. Much of the hedge accounting pronouncements are still being reviewed but guidance has been issued for immediate implementation.
